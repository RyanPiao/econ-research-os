## Conventions
- Subscript i: individual
- Subscript t: time period
- Subscript g: group
- Superscript (1), (0): potential outcomes
- Hat (^): estimated quantity
- Bar (ˉ): sample mean
- Bold: vectors and matrices
- Calligraphic: sets
